Online Store Management Application

A complete solution for those retail traders who are eager for market expansion at affordable cost. This solution includes an online store to display the whole range of products in a very user friendly way. It also includes modules to manage Delivery, Inventory, Purchases, Purchases & Sales Return.

An automated system facilitates to manage the basic accounting without any manual effort.

An integrated payment gateway can create a healthy cash flow position. This application also allows to manage offline (counter) sales from same inventory.
