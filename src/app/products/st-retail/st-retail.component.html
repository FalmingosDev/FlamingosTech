Standalone Retail Outlet (POS) Management System

  • This cloud based software application is capable to manage the entire operations of any kind of Retail Outlet.
  • It manages Purchases, Purchases Return, Vendor Accounts, Counter Sales, Sales Return, Customer Accounts, Receipts, Payments & Inventory.
  • This application is capable to handle multiple billing counters within a single retail store.
  • As this is a cloud based application hence the business owner can monitor the regular activities and reports from anywhere in the world on real time.
