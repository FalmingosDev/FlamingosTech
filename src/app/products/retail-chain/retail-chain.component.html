Retail Chain with Inventory Management System

  • This cloud based software application allows to manage retail outlets situated at different locations under one ownership along with multiple stock points.
  • Even a centralised control system with hierarchy of the users can control and monitor the entire operations.
  • It manages Purchases, Purchases Return, Vendor Accounts, Counter Sales, Sales Return, Customer Accounts, Receipts, Payments & Inventory.
  • Real time reporting system with analytical interpretation enables the management to monitor regular activities at intensive level from anywhere in the globe.
